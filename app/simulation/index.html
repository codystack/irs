Tabletop Testing

In disaster recovery, a tabletop test is a planning exercise in which organisations
respond to simulated disruption by walking through their recovery plans.
Below are three disaster scenarios. Answer the provided questions
with information from the runbook to reveal the appropriate recovery
steps for each situation.

Tutorial
Fire in the Server Room

Learn how the tabletop interface works by helping a small manufacturing company recover from an on-site hazard. Follow the tutorial to evacuate your users to safety and your systems back online.
